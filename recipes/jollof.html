Jollof

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Sunflower oil
  • Onions and peas for garnishing

Steps

  1. Add seasoning to cut chicken and steam for 15mins
  2. Fry steamed chicken using sunflower oil
  3. Fry blended tomatoes and add seasoning and salt to taste
  4. Add rice to tomato sauce, add water and set heat to low for 30mins
  5. Put peas on the cooking rice to soften it
  6. Dish out rice and garnish with peas and chopped onions
  7. Add chicken
